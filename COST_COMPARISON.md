## 💡 Cost-Saving Tips

1. **Use Gemini for free** - All tiers are free with rate limits
2. **Upgrade only if needed** - Free tier handles most use cases
3. **Batch translations** - Process multiple at once to hit rate limits efficiently
4. **Mix providers** - Use free Gemini for daily work, paid APIs only when needed
5. **Monitor usage** - Track token consumption to predict costs

---

## 🔄 When to Upgrade from Free Tier

| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|---------------|
| < 1000 translations/month | **Stay on Gemini Free** ✅ |
| 1000-5000 translations/month | **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** or **Gemini Free + occasional GPT-3.5** |
| > 5000 translations/month | **Evaluate team costs, consider bulk pricing** |
| Need premium quality | **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** or **GPT-4 Turbo** |
| High volume, budget critical | **Gemini Free tier always** |
